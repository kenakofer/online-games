Play Dutch Blitz

You can create or join a game of Dutch Blitz from here. You can make up a game room, but you and your friends must use the same game room to play with each other. Powerusers will note that you can bypass this page by directly visiting a url in the format below.

games.kenakofer.com / ?num= &AI=

Dutch Blitz Rules

Drag cards from your QUEUE and DUMP piles into the play area, where you can build stacks that start with 1 and go to 10 in each color. Click on your deck to deal 3 cards at a time into your dump. When the deck is gone, you can click on the DECK space again to put the dump cards back.

The game is over when one of the players has depleted the pile at the left of their queue. Your score is the number of cards you played minus 2 times the number of cards left in this pile.
